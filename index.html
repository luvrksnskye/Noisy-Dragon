My Services

FULL COLORED SKETCH

FROM 100$ TO 400$

Elaborated full color sketch, adding shadows and partial lighting, with a simple patterned background commission. Max 3 characters. Depending on the complexity of the characters and number of elements in the sheet, I can take from one week to one month!

SCENERY COMMISSION

FROM 95$ TO 550$

Full scenery with shades/lights and elaborate background, the price varies between headshots, half-bodys and full-bodys. Max 2 characters. Delivery time depends on the complexity of the commission.

3-POSES SKETCH PAGE

FROM 195$ TO 300$

Your character in three different poses, you can choose the poses or give me creative freedom, the price range can include nsfw and sfw as well as alt versions. Delivery time depends on the complexity of the character.

REFERENCE SHEET

FROM 150$ TO 435$

Customized reference sheet according to the client's needs, it is handled in a sketchy style without shading, quite colorful. NSFW versions are included within the price range.

DRAGONIZE YOUR CHARACTER!

FROM 80$ TO 125$

I will draw your oc turned into a dragon in a full sketch with color. Depending on the complexity of the character and the number of elements on the drawing, the price varies.

EXPERIMENTAL COMMISSIONS

FROM 65$ TO 255$

These will be experimental commissions just for warm-up purposes, they will usually be delivered very quickly, the price varies depending on the style and complexity.

Terms of Service

  • PAYMENT PLANS ARE AVAILABLE IN ANY COMM UP FROM $65!
  • The delivery time depends on your Trello queue position.
  • Have a clear idea when commissioning unless choosing creative freedom.
  • Payment is required in advance; no refunds after one week except in emergencies.
  • Be very specific; references, sketches, and details help.
  • Private commissions require an extra cost.
  • Commissions are sent via email, Telegram, or Discord.
  • No major changes after sketch approval; minor changes depend on complexity.
  • Always check the Trello queue for commission status.
  • No deadline commissions, except for black and white sketches or lineart.
  • Check the price table; complex subjects may cost more.
  • Do not pressure me; I handle multiple commissions at a time.
  • Updates are provided during commission progress.
  • If I don't reply quickly, I am busy working.
  • No commissions for merch or profit without commercial rights purchase.
  • Delivery time varies based on complexity.
  • Contact my SFW or NSFW account accordingly.
  • I reserve the right to refuse commissions that make me uncomfortable.
  • Character designs will be drawn as provided; no major modifications.
